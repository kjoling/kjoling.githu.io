About Me

Hi! I'm Keean Joling, a self-taught programmer who has a BS in Kinesiology and an MA in Science Education. Currently, I work as a Management Analyst for Washington State in public records.


I provide technical assistance to my unit, generate reports, provide analysis, test new features, write how-to guides, and am part of a team that ensures compliance with WA RCW regarding disclosure of public records.


In my free time I like to workout, spend time with friends and family, play video games, enjoy live music, and recently picked up learning how to DJ. I consider myself a life-long learner, always trying to improve myself in pursuit of being the best version of myself that I can be.


Fun fact! I've been programing for 6,506,307,255 seconds!
